FAQ

Our most frequently asked questions

What is your listing commission?

The standard is 2.5%. We charge 1% for the listing (or selling) side.

Do you really charge 1% on the listing end?

Yes. With the rise of technology and changing landscape of the real estate market, it's easier to list your home and sell it for excellent market value.

Are there any upfront fees to list our home?

None! Listing your home doesn’t require any upfront costs and you still get low commission, professional real estate services.

Should I buy or should I sell my home first?

Having proceeds from a recent sale on hand will certainly make financing your next purchase easier. That’s one reason you might want to sell first.
